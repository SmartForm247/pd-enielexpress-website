Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your shipment by entering your tracking number on our tracking page. You can find the tracking number on your receipt or in the confirmation email. We also provide real-time updates via WhatsApp if you've opted in for notifications.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and online payments through Paystack. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.

International shipping times vary depending on the destination and service type selected. Express international shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while standard international shipping can take 7-14 business days. Customs clearance may affect delivery times.

If your package arrives damaged, please contact our customer service immediately with your tracking number and photos of the damage. We'll guide you through the claims process and work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Address changes may be possible depending on the shipment status. Please contact our customer service as soon as possible with your tracking number. Additional fees may apply for address changes, especially for international shipments.

Yes, we offer insurance options for your shipments. Basic coverage is included with most services, and additional insurance can be purchased for high-value items. Please contact our sales team for more information on insurance options.
